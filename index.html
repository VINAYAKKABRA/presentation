Established in 1947 as Shri Krishna Metal, we have built a legacy spanning over 75 years as leading Manufacturers and Exporters of Brass Products.

We manufacture Brass Products. Brass is an Alloy, i.e. Mixture of Copper and Zinc. It versatile metal used in various applications, including construction, engineering, and automotive industries.

Operating from Bhandara, Maharashtra, we handle bulk orders with timely delivery, cementing our reputation as a trusted partner.
